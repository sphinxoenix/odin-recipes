Maple Salmon

Description

This maple glazed salmon is delicious and very easy to prepare. It's easy to make with simple ingreidents you probably already have on hand.

Ingredients

  • Maple Syrup
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
    • Garlic Salt
    • Ground Black Pepper
  • Salmon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Stir maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt, and pepper together in a small bowl.
  3. Cut salmon into four equal-sized fillets; place in a shallow glass baking dish and coat with maple syrup mixture. Cover the dish and marinate salmon in the refigerator for thirty minutes, turning once halfway.
  4. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake salmon uncovered until flesh flakes with a fork, about twenty minutes.
